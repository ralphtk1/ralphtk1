## 🚀 Mindset
I approach networking and security with the understanding that systems don’t fail randomly — there’s always a reason, and it’s my job to find it. I focus on learning how traffic flows, how access is controlled, and how small misconfigurations can impact entire environments.

Coming from a background where I had to build everything step by step. Whether it’s troubleshooting DNS issues, validating connectivity, or working through access control in Active Directory and Azure AD, I’m always trying to connect the “why” behind what I’m doing.

My goal is to build a strong foundation in networking and use that to move into security, where understanding how systems communicate and fail is critical. I’m not just trying to learn tools — I’m building the mindset to analyze, troubleshoot, and secure real environments over the long term.
